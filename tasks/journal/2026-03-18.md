## HDR compositing bug (confirmed)

Neon glow looked dim in burned video but bright in browser. Suspected node-canvas `shadowBlur` weakness, but frame inspection (`tasks/render_frames.mjs`) showed frames looked fine.

**Root cause confirmed:** canvas overlay is SDR. When composited onto HDR (BT.2020 HLG) video, glow values can't reach HDR peak brightness — looks washed out.

**Fix:** Auto-detect HDR from `color_transfer` field in ffprobe output. If HDR, apply tonemap filter in ffmpeg (`zscale` + `tonemap=hable`) before overlay, and encode output as x264/SDR instead of x265/HDR. Side effect: ~3x faster encoding.
